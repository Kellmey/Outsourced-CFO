FAQs

Everything you need to know.

What services does Outsourced CFO offer?
Four pillars: CFO Services (strategic finance, forecasting, board reporting, capital raising and business valuations), Cloud Accounting (bookkeeping, tax, payroll, VAT, IFRS), Automation Services (Zoho, Xero, Zapier and tech-stack integration), and Talent Solutions (interim and permanent finance hires).
Who do you work with?
Founders and growing companies — from startups to listed businesses — across 34+ countries and more than 20 industries, including SaaS, fintech, medtech, e-commerce, manufacturing, professional services, creative agencies, venture capital and impact ventures.
How is this different from a bookkeeper or accountant?
A bookkeeper records what happened. An accountant files taxes on it. An Outsourced CFO uses that data to tell you what to do next — pricing, hiring, fundraising, investment. We can work alongside your existing accountants or fully replace the function, depending on what you need.
What cloud systems do you work with?
We're certified advisors and implementation partners for a leading cloud tech stack including Xero, Zoho One, Zapier, Unleashed, Dext, Shopify, Vend, Spotlight and SimplePay — so we can meet you where you are or recommend the right setup for where you're going.
How do you get started with a new client?
Get in touch and jump on a call with our team. We'll talk through your business and current finance setup, then propose a tailored engagement — the right mix of CFO, accounting, automation and talent for your stage and goals.
Where are you based?
Our US office is at 260 Madison Avenue, New York City, NY 10016. We serve founders and management teams across 34+ countries, with dedicated teams matched to each client.
